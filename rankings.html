📐 How We Rank

Rankings are compiled every Monday using a weighted composite of public benchmark scores, community Elo ratings from LMSYS Chatbot Arena, and verified test results from our own eval suite. Scores are normalized to a 0–100 scale.

🧠 Reasoning 30%

MMLU, HellaSwag, ARC-Challenge, BIG-Bench Hard. Tests multi-step logic and world knowledge.

💻 Coding 25%

HumanEval, MBPP, LiveCodeBench. Measures code generation correctness and debugging ability.

👁️ Vision 15%

MMMU, DocVQA, ChartQA. Only applicable to multimodal models; non-vision models score 0.

✍️ Writing 15%

MT-Bench creative writing, AlpacaEval 2.0. Evaluated against human preference ratings.

⚡ Speed + Cost 10%

Tokens/sec throughput and $/1M token pricing. Normalised across hosting tiers.

🏟️ Arena Elo 5%

Live LMSYS Chatbot Arena Elo score from real human preference battles.
